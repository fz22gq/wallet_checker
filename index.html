Wallet Checker Help

Discover your Songbird and Flare wallet information effortlessly with our Telegram bot.
Simply use intuitive commands to access balances, rewards, and more, all in one convenient platform.

  • /help - Displays explanation of each bot command.

Admin:

  • /clearaddress - Clears previously set address.
  • /setaddress - Sets wallet address, remains set until cleared.

Balances:

  • /balance - Checks the SGB, WSGB, FLR, WFLR balance of address.
  • /balancewflrblock [block] - Check the WFLR balance of address on Flare at specified block.
  • /balancewsgbblock [block] - Check the WSGB balance of address on Songbird at specified block.

FlareDrops

  • /flaredrops - Lists all FlareDrops claimed with month, amount, date and time for address.

FTSO Rewards:

  • /unclaimedepochs - Lists all epochs with unclaimed SGB & FLR rewards for address.
  • /unclaimedrewards - Lists all unclaimed SGB & FLR rewards by epoch, with unclaimed amount.

Validator Rewards:

  • /validatorrewards - Lists total, claimed and unclaimed rewards for address.
